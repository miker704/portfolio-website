Centipede.js

Centipede.js is a recreation of the classic Atari 1981 game Centipede with a huge amount of custom twists, besides including the features of the classic game, Centipede.js includes a large variety of new enemies, power ups, and abilities to obtain and use. $TR!F3

$TR!F3

STRIFE stylized as '$TR!F3' is a full-stack, comprehensive clone of the popular communication app Discord and it's myriad of functionalities. Users are able to sign up for an account, join servers, and chat with other users in real time via text message, or voice/video calls. $TR!F3 utlizes its own engine/api called CORE to handle many of its async capabilities and to 'Hack' around the caveats of Action Cable, and allow constant data flow of websockets, it utilizes a combination of Action Cable and Redux to control the many async features in the app that are not hooked by a cable allowing for a fully async app. Some notable technologies used in this project are: React.js, Redux, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, PostgresSQL, CoffeeScript, C++, WebRTC, AWS, Redis.
